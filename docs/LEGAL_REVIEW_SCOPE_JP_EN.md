# StockWaveJP Legal Review Scope

The legal review should cover more than the Terms of Service, Privacy Policy and Disclaimer.

## A. Core legal documents
- Terms of Service
- Privacy Policy
- Investment/market-data Disclaimer
- Specified Commercial Transactions Act disclosure
- Subscription cancellation and refund policy
- Cookie and external-transmission notice
- Copyright and data-source policy

## B. Purchase and subscription flow
Review the Plan & Pricing page, trial explanation, checkout button text, Stripe checkout screen, final confirmation screen, renewal timing, billing cycle, tax-inclusive price display, automatic renewal, cancellation deadline, effective cancellation date, refunds, failed payments and plan downgrade.

Confirm that the user can review and correct the order before submission.

## C. Financial-regulation boundary
Review whether rankings, focus labels, alerts, columns, portfolio-like features, individualized notifications or paid features could be interpreted as investment advice rather than general market information.

Review wording such as “recommended,” “buy signal,” “winning stock,” “expected return,” “target price” and personalized suggestions.

## D. Data, intellectual property and provider contracts
- Infoway licence scope, commercial display and redistribution
- Exchange/index/ETF names and trademarks
- EDINET data use
- Company logos and screenshots
- Article quotations and source attribution
- User-created custom-theme data
- Prohibition on scraping, bulk download and resale

## E. Personal information and security
- Google/Supabase authentication data
- Stripe customer and payment identifiers
- Contact-form data
- Analytics, logs, IP addresses and cookies
- Data-retention periods
- Overseas processors and foreign transfers
- Data deletion and account closure
- Disclosure/correction/suspension request procedure
- Security measures and incident response
- Processor/vendor supervision

## F. Advertising and affiliate disclosure
- AdSense or other advertisements
- Affiliate links
- Sponsored columns
- Conflicts of interest
- Holdings or positions of the operator
- Clear advertising labels

## G. Operational pages and claims
- About
- How to Use
- Plan & Pricing
- Settings/account deletion
- Contact
- News/announcements
- Data-update labels
- “Real-time,” “latest,” “No.1,” accuracy and performance claims
- Free-trial and feature-comparison claims

## H. English version
- Governing law and jurisdiction for overseas users
- Whether the service is intended for users outside Japan
- Privacy disclosures for overseas processors/users
- Currency conversion and ADR explanations
- English translation controlling-language clause
- Consumer-law limitations by jurisdiction

## I. Internal operational documents
These need not all be public, but counsel should review whether they are sufficient:
- Personal-data handling rules
- Access-control policy
- Incident-response procedure
- Vendor/processor list
- Data-retention and deletion schedule
- Content correction policy
- Conflict-of-interest policy
- Advertising and sponsorship policy
